## Builders Bounty

The Builders Bounty program maintains a curated list of opportunities for innovative developers to contribute to the Ronin ecosystem through our [Builders Grant initiative](https://www.notion.so/skymavis/Builders-Grant-15ac48ae3fdd80219af1c8c45d2ebdb7). This program rewards builders who tackle key challenges and opportunities within our ecosystem. 

### How to participate in the Builders Bounty?

Browse available opportunities on our [Ideas Board](https://github.com/orgs/ronin-chain/projects/1). Our team reviews and categorizes each idea into one of three states:

* Open - Available opportunities that anyone can tackle
* In Progress - Opportunities currently being developed by builders
* Completed - Successfully implemented solutions
  
**Steps**

1. Choose an open bounty that interests you
2. Comment on the bounty to indicate your intention to tackle it 
3. Submit your application through our [Grants Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1d-h2C1ZcbA_KSpFdAmCfPzRKbgz1NV8AmOURiER0tOw/edit), including a reference to your chosen bounty
